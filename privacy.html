Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 08, 2025

At One Click Productivity ("We", "Us", or "Our"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Definition of Personal Data

Personal Data means any information that identifies you as an individual, such as your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or other information that can be used to identify you.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear: We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users of our application. Your use of the app does not require you to provide any personal information.

Permissions and Why We Use Them

To provide key features of our app, we request the following permissions:

  • Foreground Service Permission: This allows the app to run certain background tasks reliably, in this case for the recurring todos functionality, ensuring uninterrupted functionality. This is a normal-level permission automatically granted by the system at install time and does not require user approval during app use.
  • Query All Packages Permission: This permission lets the app detect which other apps are installed on your device. It is necessary to select the apps affected by appblocking. This is a normal-level permission automatically granted by the system at install time and does not require user approval during app use.
  • System Alert Window Permission: This permission allows the app to display overlays on top of other apps, in this case the "window/overlay" that appears when a blocked app is opened. This permission can be removed at any point manually in the system settings.
  • Accessibility Service Permission: This permission enables the app to monitor and interact with the device's user interface, in this case to detect when a blocked app is opened and to close the app when the "Close-Button" on the "Overlay/Block - Window" is pressed. This permission can be removed at any point manually in the system settings.

We use these permissions only to deliver the described features above and do not use them to collect or share your personal information.
